Title: Listening Habits by Genre, Mood, Activity, and Duration

Description
I recorded two weeks of personal music listening and documenting genre, mood,
activity, time of day, and date of music listened to.

Format
There are 47 rows, not including the headers, and there are 6 columns. 
  Genre
    Genre of music.
  Mood  
    How I was feeling.
  Activity
    What I was doing at the time.
  Time of Day
    Time of the day measured in hours and minutes.
  Duration
    How long I listened to the music in minutes.
  Date
    Month and day of time listened.

Source
I recorded data every day into an excel sheet in an orderly format. I 
collected my data at the start of and at the end of the listening period so 
that I could record duration and start and end times.
